Commonly used equipment

Generally speaking, the main equipment in video production is the camera, but there are also other devices such as tripods, microphones, sliders, drones, green screens, etc.Here, I've listed some commonly used equipment. By clicking on them, you can visit the official websites of these equipment providers to take a look.

Nikon camera

Nikon is a well-known Japanese camera manufacturer, renowned worldwide for its high-quality photographic equipment. Nikon cameras are favored by professional photographers and photography enthusiasts for their excellent image quality, and durable design.

Camera

DJi Drones

In the field of aerial photography drones, DJI indeed stands out as a leader, with its innovation and influence in the film and television industry being particularly notable.

Drones

Microphones

Microphones play a crucial role in the film and television industry, ensuring that the audio captured matches the high production values of the visuals.

Microphones

Fill light

Fill lights in the film and television industry are used to improve lighting conditions, shape atmosphere and character images, enhance image quality, and achieve special lighting effects, thereby enhancing the visual impact and professional level of film and television productions.

Fill light

Stabilizer

The main function of a stabilizer is to reduce the shake during handheld camera shooting and to achieve smooth and stable moving shots. It enhances the fluidity and watchability of the footage, allowing photographers to capture clear and coherent images in various moving and dynamic environments.

Stabilizer

Slider

A slider provides smooth movement, allowing the camera to pan or zoom at a steady pace, which is crucial for creating fluid dynamic shots.

Slider
